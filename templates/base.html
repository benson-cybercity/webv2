Financial institutions play an important role in the Saiba platform Saiba, Inc. is an agent of Veridian Credit Union and Compass Bank and all funds associated with your account in the Saiba network are held in pooled accounts at Veridian Credit Union and Compass Bank. These funds are not eligible for individual insurance, including FDIC insurance and may not be eligible for share insurance by the National Credit Union Share Insurance Fund.
