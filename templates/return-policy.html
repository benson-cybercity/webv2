Saiba Return And Refund Policy

Saiba is committed to process all refunds within 24 hours.Please note that banks take 3-5 days to process the refunds.

Who Can Get A Refund?

Saiba gives a refund if payment is successful, and payment is credited to Saiba balance but user is not able to get airtime or pay for bills and services

How to request a refund?

Fill in the Customer Support Request in the Help section of your app. Mention your current Saiba balance and the problem you are facing. We typically respond in no more than 6 hours. Please note that banks take 3-5 days to process the refunds.
